The Agents

Six specialists.
One platform.

Each agent is purpose-built for a specific operational domain. They run independently and report to a central orchestrator.

PRICING
Pricing Intelligence

Tracks competitor prices across markets, detects margin opportunities, and flags pricing anomalies before they cost you revenue.

SEO
SEO Monitor

Monitors keyword rankings, detects traffic shifts, and surfaces content gaps with specific remediation steps.

CRO
Conversion Audit

Analyzes funnel behavior, identifies friction points, and proposes targeted experiments based on real session data.

REPORTING
Operations Report

Synthesizes GA4, ad spend, and revenue data into a weekly digest with variance analysis and trend alerts.

INVENTORY
Inventory Signals

Detects low stock risks, seasonal demand shifts, and supplier lead time mismatches before they become stockouts.

LOCALIZATION
Localization Coverage

Audits multilingual store coverage, flags untranslated content, and tracks localization gaps across target markets.
