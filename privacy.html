Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how DevsMachina ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects personal data submitted through devsmachina.app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently.

DevsMachina is operated from Serbia. By submitting your information through this website, you acknowledge that your data may be processed in accordance with this policy.

1. What data we collect

When you submit the early access or waitlist form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your store URL (optional)
  • Your IP address (collected automatically by Cloudflare Turnstile for spam prevention)

2. Why we collect it

We collect this data solely to:

  • Add you to the early access waitlist
  • Contact you about DevsMachina product updates and launch
  • Prevent spam and abuse through Cloudflare Turnstile

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the above.

3. How we store and process your data

Form submissions are processed and stored by Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form service. Formspree stores your submission data on their servers. You can review Formspree's privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

Spam prevention is handled by Cloudflare Turnstile. Cloudflare may process your IP address and browser signals to determine whether a form submission is human. You can review Cloudflare's privacy policy at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

This website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages and fonts are loaded from Google Fonts. Google may log your IP address when fonts are requested. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

4. How long we keep your data

We retain your data for as long as necessary to manage the waitlist and notify you about the product launch. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

5. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@devsmachina.app.

6. Cookies

This website does not use tracking or analytics cookies. Cloudflare Turnstile may set a functional cookie necessary for spam prevention. Google Fonts may set a cookie when fonts are loaded. These are technical necessities and do not track your browsing behavior across other sites.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Any significant changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@devsmachina.app.
